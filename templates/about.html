About us

Youth Aid Education Possible Changes Organization

WYAPO is further committed to work with poor communities in Tanzania to promote, children, youth, women's dignity and rights. Currently, YAPO is operating and implementing projects in Morogoro region (Gairo and Kilosa districts), Dar es Salaam.

YAPO is governed by the Board of Directors who oversees all the operations under the Executive Director, the staff and volunteers.

The organization is mandated to work in Tanzania Mainland. The organization Head office located at Pwani Street, near Tegeta Nyuki Market in Kinondoni district in Dar es Salaam.
