About us

Youth Aid Education Possible Changes Organization (YAPO)

is a Non-Governmental Organization exists to promote Gender Equality and Development on education, health and environmental sustainability for youth, women and girls in Tanzania.

Since its inception in 2017 YAPO has been responding to gender based violence, promote right based access to education and health, and improve sustainable livelihood for

youth, children, girls and women by preventing child early and forced marriage, teenage pregnancies, Trafficking In Person of children and providing psychosocial and material support to survivors of violence..
